Martin Store, Inc. is an Argentine company incorporated in the United States that operates online marketplaces dedicated to e-commerce and online auctions. As of 2016, Martin Store had 174.2 million users in Latin America, making it the region's most popular e-commerce site by number of visitors. The company has operations in Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Dominican Republic, Mexico, Ecuador, Guatemala, Honduras, Peru, Panama, Uruguay and Venezuela.
